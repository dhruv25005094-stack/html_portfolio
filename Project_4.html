Our Mission

समाज में स्थायी परिवर्तन लाना

Samarpan Sewa was founded in 2015 with a powerful vision: to empower rural and urban poor communities across India by providing quality education, clean water, and sustainable livelihood opportunities.

We believe that real change happens when people are equipped with the tools and knowledge to improve their own lives. Our approach focuses on sustainable solutions that create long-term impact rather than temporary relief.

To date, we've impacted over 75,000 lives across 18 Indian states, built 42 schools and learning centers, provided clean water access to 250+ villages, trained 8,000 individuals in skills like tailoring, carpentry, and digital literacy.
